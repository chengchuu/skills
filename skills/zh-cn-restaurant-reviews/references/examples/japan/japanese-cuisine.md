## Example: 奈良木质小食堂

- Country: Japan
- Region: Nara
- Category: Japanese cuisine
- Cuisine: Japanese set meals
- Platform: unknown
- Sentiment: Positive
- Tone: Descriptive
- Length: Standard
- Occasion: unknown
- Main topics: Location, decor, dishes, freshness, clientele
- Recommended dishes: 定食
- Source: `temp/examples/25-0307-1332-Food-Shokudo.md`

### Available facts

- 离近铁奈良站近，店内有木质小屋感和小庭院。
- 定食配菜丰富且新鲜。

### Review

奈良这家食堂离近铁奈良站挺近，走几分钟就到了。店不算大，但有种很舒服的木质小屋感觉，还带个小庭院，坐下来莫名让人安静不少。点的定食配菜丰富，不是随便摆摆那种，主食和小菜都能吃出来挺新鲜。感觉游客没那么多，更像本地人会来的小食堂。是会想慢慢吃完的那种小店。

## Example: 熊本连锁日式定食

- Country: Japan
- Region: Kumamoto
- Category: Japanese cuisine
- Cuisine: Japanese set meals
- Platform: unknown
- Sentiment: Positive
- Tone: Natural conversational
- Length: Standard
- Occasion: Quick meal
- Main topics: Ordering, dishes, portion, service
- Recommended dishes: 日式定食
- Source: `temp/examples/25-0608-1855-Food-弥生轩.md`

### Available facts

- 门口机器自助点餐。
- 套餐小菜多，有饭有面，分量刚好。
