## Example: 排查 Docker 权限错误

- Document type: Troubleshooting guide
- Subject: Docker and containers
- Audience: Developers and system administrators
- Tone: Diagnostic and restrained
- Formality: Medium to high
- Length: Short
- Source: `temp/writing-examples/Docker/23-0119_Docker.md`; `temp/writing-examples/TCloud/23-0119_Docker_TCloud.md`

### Structural characteristics

- 从症状和环境出发，依次组织诊断、原因、修复、验证和预防措施。
- 从明确错误信息进入权限原因、修复命令和验证。

### Reusable style characteristics

- 结论以证据为基础；将错误信息、命令和输出与解释分开。
- 保留原始错误与命令，说明为何修复有效。
