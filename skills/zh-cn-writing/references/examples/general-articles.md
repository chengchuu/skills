# General article examples

These normalized examples capture reusable structure and style without reproducing distinctive source prose. Source articles are not factual authorities.

## Example: 关于快乐的概念文章

- Document type: General article
- Subject: General concepts
- Audience: General readers
- Tone: Conversational and reflective
- Formality: Medium to high
- Length: Medium
- Source: `temp/writing-examples/Pages/26-0424_Happy.md`

### Structural characteristics

- 围绕一个中心问题组织观点、例子和结论，不照搬技术操作文档的固定结构。
- 围绕中心问题分段讨论不同视角和实际含义。

### Reusable style characteristics

- 保持清楚、克制和可读；区分个人观察与可验证事实。
- 可借鉴清楚的分段与总结，不把个人观点转为技术写作规则。
